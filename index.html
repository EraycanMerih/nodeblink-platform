What Is NodeBlink?

A simple checkout system for digital commerce

NodeBlink sits between your audience and your product. It turns an offer into a single shareable checkout link with pricing, payment, receipts, and delivery all handled in one flow.

For Creators

Sell files, templates, memberships, or services without building a full storefront.

For Teams

Run campaigns with measurable clicks, conversions, receipts, and fulfillment status.

For Buyers

See a clear offer, pay once, and get instant confirmation with delivery or receipt tracking.

Platform Features

Everything needed to operate confidently

  • Create Blinks for digital assets or service actions.
  • Upload a banner image so each Blink looks like a real product page.
  • Set stock, pricing, and delivery rules without extra tooling.
  • Track clicks, sales, revenue, receipts, and delivery status in one place.
  • Keep large Blink catalogs organized with pagination.

How It Works

From click to delivery in four steps

The flow is intentionally plain: create a Blink, share it, let the buyer pay, then send the file or trigger the service automatically. No extra storefront is required.

Step 1

Create Blink

Add the title, price, stock, and upload the file or service details.

Step 2

Share Anywhere

Post the Blink link on social platforms, communities, or direct messages.

Step 3

Buyer Pays

The buyer reviews the offer, pays from their wallet, and gets confirmation.

Step 4

Delivery Happens

The asset link is issued or the webhook fires automatically.

Delivery

Digital delivery or automated actions

NodeBlink supports two clear paths. If you sell a file, the buyer gets a secure download. If you sell a service, NodeBlink can trigger your webhook so the action happens automatically.

  • Secure download links for digital assets.
  • Webhook-based fulfillment for services and automation.
  • Receipts and transaction records for support and auditing.

FAQ

Most asked questions

What does NodeBlink do?

It turns a product or service into a simple checkout link that can be shared anywhere online.

Can I sell unlimited stock?

Yes. Set stock to 0 and the Blink stays available without running out.

Can I upload a banner?

Yes. The dashboard supports banner image uploads for richer product previews.

Do you hold user funds?

No. NodeBlink is non-custodial and routes payment directly through the wallet flow.

How are purchases delivered?

Digital assets are delivered through secure download links and service offers can trigger a webhook automatically.

Simple pricing

Protocol fee: 1.5% per successful transaction. No confusing settings to manage.
